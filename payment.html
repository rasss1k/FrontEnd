Payment and delivery
The company LLC "DEALER" equipment, components, tools and machine tools are easy to order, pay and receive. On this page we have collected information about delivery and payment.
How to pay?
Payment is accepted by bank transfer. After the order is approved, we issue an invoice for payment. The order is considered paid after receipt of the order amount to the settlement account of LLC "DEALER".
How to get it?
The geography of delivery is the whole of Qazaqstan. When transporting goods, they are subject to insurance. Usually the delivery is included in the price of the products. However, if you prefer to work with another courier service, we have no restrictions. Just let us know. We will agree on all the conditions and send the order with a full set of related documentation.
